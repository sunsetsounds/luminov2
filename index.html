Portable High Fidelity Audio Reproduction

Design Philosophy and Overview


Lumino+ and Lumino are portable HiFi speakers that place a strong emphasis on sound quality and attention to detail. Before Lumino, high fidelity audio systems were confined to static home setups to provide outstanding listening experience. Portable speakers sacrifice sound quality and attention to detail for the sake of convenience and high unit production in a fast market. Sunset Sounds has developed and engineered Lumino, an audio solution which brings out the best in HiFi and portable speakers by putting superior sound reproduction in a portable form factor. Lumino is also 3D printed to allow for a unique design and handcrafted for the best product finish and care. For the first time, you can bring HiFi quality audio wherever you go!

At a Glance

Some specifications and numbers to convince you that our formula works.

  • 35Hz- 20kHz Response
  • 2x50W (1% THD+N) Output
  • System F3: 43Hz
  • 40 Hour Battery Life*
  • 100dB Peak Output
  • 5.00 lbs (2.27 kg)

*40 hour battery estimate is for Lumino+, Lumino has 20 hours of estimated playtime.
