A two way system Built for HiFi

Lumino v2 features a high-performance, long-excursion, 5.25" (140mm) aluminum-cone main woofer capable of reproducing fast transients and deep, accurate bass when coupled with our precision-tuned quad-array of 3.5" (90mm) passive radiators. A 1.125" (25mm) soft dome tweeter complements the woofer, providing extremely flat high-frequency response and amazing detail with minimal distortion. Both the tweeter and the woofer have neodymium magnets for improved efficiency, magnetic core balance, and reduced weight. Together, Lumino v2 delivers a complete soundstage with commanding low-frequency bass response and crystal-clear mid-high dynamics.

No-compromise HiFi Audio Performance

Lumino v2 was designed with high fidelity audio reproduction in mind. Delivering the most transparent output is important in reproducing music samples exactly like the artist intended. For this reason, we have spent obsessive amounts of time engineering and tuning Lumino to be the ultimate portable canvas for their work. The audio performance of Lumino v2 is shown through our dedication to perfecting its response characteristics: A maximally flat response from 50Hz - 22kHz, an extremely low F3 of 43 Hz, and well-controlled distortion figures.
