Lumino+ and Lumino are portable HiFi speakers that place a strong emphasis on sound quality and attention to detail. Before Lumino, high fidelity audio systems were confined to static home setups to provide outstanding listening experience. Portable speakers sacrifice sound quality and attention to detail for the sake of convenience and high unit production in a fast market. Sunset Sounds has developed and engineered Lumino, an audio solution which brings out the best in HiFi and portable speakers by putting superior sound reproduction in a portable form factor. Lumino is also 3D printed to allow for a unique design and handcrafted for the best product finish and care. For the first time, you can bring HiFi quality audio wherever you go!

At a Glance

Key specifications and numbers to convince you that our formula works.

  • 35Hz - 22kHz Response
  • 2x50W (1% THD+N) Output
  • System F3: 43Hz*
  • 50 Hour Battery Life**
  • 100dB Max Output
  • 5.00 lbs (2.27 kg)

*F3 is characteristic of a speaker's bass. It is the frequency achieved at -3dB from nominal. The lower, the better.
**A modest estimate. Playtime can drastically improve at lower volumes.

Drivers Built for HiFi

Lumino v2 features a high-performance, long-excursion, 5.25" (140mm) aluminum-cone main woofer capable of reproducing fast transients and deep, accurate bass when coupled with our precision-tuned quad-array of 3.5" (90mm) passive radiators. A 1.125" (25mm) soft dome tweeter complements the woofer, providing extremely flat high-frequency response and amazing detail with minimal distortion. Together, Lumino v2 delivers a complete soundstage with dreamy low-frequency bass response and crystal-clear dynamics.

Transparent HiFi Audio Performance

PETG, used on the bottom half of Lumino+ and the entirety of Lumino's enclosure, is a strong and flexible material, making it more effective at protection when compared to PLA. Much like PLA, PETG is recyclable but is not biodegradable because it is oil based. Still being recyclable, PETG is still a sustainable choice of materials when treated properly and we are currently working on implementing a biodegradable PETG solution!

Traditional and Contemporary Design Language

TPU is an extremely flexible filament that is used for Lumino's handle. This material with its high elasticity, shear strength, and flexural strength give the handle a high quality and robust feel. TPU is sustainable choice because of its decent biodegradability. This material is recyclable, but Sunset Sounds is working on a plant-based TPU alternative to allow for industrial compostability!
